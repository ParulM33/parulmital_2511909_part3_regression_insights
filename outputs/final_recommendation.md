# Final Recommendation

 

## Key Drivers of Sales 

 

The analysis shows that the following factors strongly influence sales:

 

- Footfall 

- Marketing spend 

- Inventory availability 

- Staff count 

- Customer ratings 

 

---

 

## What Leadership Should Focus On 

 

1. Increase customer footfall through marketing 

2. Ensure high inventory availability 

3. Optimize marketing spend effectiveness 

4. Improve customer experience 

5. Maintain efficient staffing 

 

---

 

## Variables to Be Careful With 

 

- Discount (can reduce profit if too high) 

- Some variables with weak statistical evidence 

 

---

 

## Business Actions Recommended 

 

- Increase footfall through promotions 

- Improve stock levels to avoid lost sales 

- Reduce unnecessary discounts 

- Focus on high-performing store types 

- Analyze high-performing stores for best practices 

 

---

 

## Risks / Limitations 

 

- Model does not include external factors 

- Results show relationships, not cause 

- Market conditions may vary 

 

---

 

## Important Note 

 

Regression shows association, not causation. 

Business decisions should combine data with practical judgment.
