# Residual Analysis

 

## Purpose 

Residual analysis helps identify how actual sales differ from predicted sales.

 

Residual = Actual Sales - Predicted Sales

 

---

 

## Positive Residuals 

 

These stores performed better than expected:

- Possible reasons: good local demand, promotions, strong execution

 

---

 

## Negative Residuals 

 

These stores performed worse than expected:

- Possible reasons: stock issues, competition, poor execution

 

---

 

## Business Interpretation 

 

Positive residuals:

- Opportunity to replicate successful strategies

 

Negative residuals:

- Need investigation and corrective action

 

---

 

## Model Behavior 

 

The model may:

- Under-predict high-performing stores 

- Over-predict weaker stores 

 

---

 

## Conclusion 

 

Residuals help identify outliers and improve decision-making.
